Bayer-Monsanto M&A Negotiation

Mandy Barton, Kamil Krukowski, Nick Curran, Meghal Bhatt, Cihan Koseoglu

Background

In May 2016, German chemical giant Bayer, approached the U.S. seed giant Monsanto with a $62 billion offer to merge the two companies.

Background

The bid has increased to $66 billion in an all cash deal valued at $128 per share.

If approved, it will be the largest merger of 2016.

Germans appreciate directness and clarity and suspicious of vagueness
